Birthday chocolate cake

Makes a double layered 10" cake

Ingredients

Wet ingredients:

  • 130 gr (1.125 cup) nesquick chocolate powder
  • 40 gr (0.375 cup) cocoa powder
  • 1.5 tsp instant coffee
  • ¾ cup of boiling water
  • 2¼ cups water
  • 450 gr (2¼ cup) sugar
  • 1½ cups of oil (canola)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1½ tsp vanila

Dry ingredients:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1½ tbsp baking powder
  • ¾ tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350°F
  2. Grease two 10" cake pans
  3. In a heat-proof bowl mix nesquick powder, cocoa powder, instant coffee
  4. Add boiling water and mix until all is dissolved
  5. Add remaining wet ingredients: water, sugar, oil, eggs, and vanila (I like to beat the eggs and add the oil to creat an emulssion, totally optional)
  6. Mix dry ingredients and then combine with the wet ingredients
  7. Evenly distribute the mix between two 10" cake pans, making sure not to fill more than half the height of the pan
  8. Bake for about 30 minutes, or until tester comes out dry
  9. Let cool to room temp, assemble, and decorate
