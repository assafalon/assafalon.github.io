Elinor's Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • 1½ Oz Bourbon/Rye
  • 1 Oz simple syrup
  • ¾ Oz lemon juice (½ lemon)
  • 1 egg white

Instructions

  1. Shake dry (w/o ice)
  2. Shake wet (w/ ice)
  3. Strain into an old fashioned glass (lowball) filled with ice
