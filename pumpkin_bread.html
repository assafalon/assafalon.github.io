Pumpkin Bread

Makes 3 loaves

Ingredients

Dry ingredients:

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • 1½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp nutmeg
  • ¼ cloves

Wet ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 small sugar pumpkin, roasted and pureed (see instructions below)
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1 stick unsalted butter, melted

Roast the pumpkin

  • heat oven to 400°F
  • split pumpkin and scoop out seeds
  • lay flat-side down on parchment-lined baking sheet
  • roast for 35-45 minutes
  • let cool
  • peel off skin and puree

Instructions

  1. heat oven to 350°F
  2. greese loaf pans
  3. mix dry
  4. mix wet
  5. mix dry into wet
  6. pour into greesed loaf pans
  7. bake for about 1 hour, until tester comes out dry
