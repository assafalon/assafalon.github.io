Classic Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the dry ingredients together.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Serve warm with syrup and toppings of choice.
