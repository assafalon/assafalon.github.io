Prosciutto Elephant Ears

Ingredients

  • Puff pastry dough
  • 100 gr prosciutto
  • 1 cup grated pecorino
  • mustard

Instructions

  1. thaw the puff pastry dough (overnight in the refrigirator if you remember)
  2. heat oven to 400°F
  3. roll out the dough to a rectangle
  4. spread mustard
  5. cover with pecorino<
  6. add prosciutto
  7. season with black pepper (no need for salt)
  8. roll from both sides until you meet in the middle
  9. chill for 30 minutes in the refrigirator
  10. cut into 1 cm ears and lay on a baking tray
  11. bake for 15 minutes or until golden
  12. let cool to room temp and store in an air-tight container
