Sourdough

Feeding the starter

Do this on day 1, right before you go to sleep (Friday)

Autolyse

On day 2 (Saturday), mix 2.5% salt, 55% water, and 90% of your flour.
Let rest for 20-30 mminutes. Check your starter

Mixing the dough

After autolyse is done, mix in the starter

Folding the dough

Every 20 minutes, fold the dough.
Do this for 3-4 times, and then let the dough proof until it doubles in size

Preshaping the dough

After the dough has doubled in size, divide it in two and preshape it.
Let it rest on the counter, uncovered, for 20-30 minutes.

Shaping the dough

After the dough is rested, we shape it into either boule or batard, depending on the banneton you have and then let it proof overnight in the refrigirator
