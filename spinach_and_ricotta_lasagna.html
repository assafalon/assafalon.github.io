Spinach and ricotta lasagna

Ingredients:

  • One jar of marinara sauce (26 ounce / 750 gr)
  • A box of lasagna noodles
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 lb ricotta cheese
  • 1 bag of Spinach, either frozen, thawed and squeezed, or fresh and wilted in a pan
  • 1 lb shredded mozzarella
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 400°F
  2. In a bowl mix:
    • Eggs
    • Ricotta cheese
    • Spinach
    • ½ Cup of the parmesan cheese
    • Oregano, salt, and pepper
  3. Cover the bottom of a pan with sauce
  4. Layer 1 and 2:
    • Lasagna noodles
    • Sauce
    • ½ of the ricotta-spinach mix
    • Mozzarella
  5. Top with lasagna noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and the remaining parmesan
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes
  7. Remove cover and bake for additional 15 minutes
