Waffles

Makes 6-7 waffles

Ingredients

Dry ingredients (sift flour and baking powder):

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt

Wet ingredients (add in this order, whisking until homogeneous after each addition ):

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 6 tbsp canola oil (75 gr)
  • 1¾ cup milk

Instructions

  1. mix dry into wet
  2. use spray oil to greese both grids of the waffle maker
  3. pour ½ cup of the batter into the bottom grid
