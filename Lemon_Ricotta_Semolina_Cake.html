Lemon Ricotta Semolina Cake

Ingredients

  • 3.5 tbsp (50g) Butter
  • 1qt (1L) Milk
  • 1 Vanilla bean (1 tsp Extract)
  • 1 Lemon Peel
  • 1¼ cup (230g) Semolina
  • 6 Eggs
  • 1½ cup (310g) Sugar
  • 1 cup (250g) Ricotta
  • Powdered sugar and Lemon for garnish

Instructions

  1. heat oven to 350°F (160°C)
  2. In a pot, heat the butter, milk, lemon peel, salt and vanilla to a simmer
  3. Strain the solids. Turn the heat down and add in the semolina
  4. Stir for a minute or two until the mixture become a mush
  5. Set aside to cool
  6. Mix together the eggs and sugar until light and creamy
  7. Mix in the ricotta and the semolina mixture until smooth
  8. Butter a springform pan and pour in the mixture
  9. Bake for 1 hour. The cake should still be a bit jiggly in the center
  10. Let the cake slowly come to room temp before removing the springform pan
  11. Top with powdered sugar, garnish with a lemon and enjoy!
