Penicillin

Ingredients

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • ¾ oz honey syrup (1:1 honey:water)
  • ¾ oz lemon juice (½ lemon)
  • ¼ oz peaty Islay whiskey (Lagavulin, Ardbeg, Laphroaig, etc.)
  • a knob of ginger

Instructions

  1. Use a muddler to crush the ginger inside the shaker
  2. Add all the ingredients (other than the Islay whiskey) to the shaker
  3. Add ice and shake
  4. Strain twice into an old fashioned glass (lowball) filled with ice
  5. Gently float the Islay whiskey on top
  6. If you want you can garnish with candied ginger - I don't
