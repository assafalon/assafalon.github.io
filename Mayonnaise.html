Mayonnaise

Ingredients

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup canola oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, using a hand blender or a whisk, mix everything but the oil
  2. Gradually add oil, at first a few drops at a time and as the emulssion forms, more repidly
