Yam casserole

Ingredients:

  • 3-5 yams or sweet potatos - peeled and sliced
  • 1½ sticks of butter (170 gr)
  • ½ cup of white sugar
  • ½ cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp nutmeg
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 500 gr ricotta
  • ½ cup roasted pecans
  • honey
  • raisins soaks in whiskey

Instructions:

  1. heat oven to 350°F
  2. mix butter and spices in a bowl
  3. mix butter mixture with yam slices in a casserole dish
  4. cover and bake for ~30 minutes
  5. remove cover, mix, and bake for an additional 15-30 minutes, until soft
  6. add ricotta, pecans, rasins
  7. drizzle with honey
